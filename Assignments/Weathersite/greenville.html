Greenville set for massive thunderstorms

Meatoroligist predict heavy thunderstorms

Metoroligist John Brackner states that the storm front that is moving into the area this weekend will bring with it a number of thunderstorms. These storms pose a grave threat to our area with the dryness and high heat that has gripped the area.

Officials have both standing and volenteer firefighters on standby to deal with any fires that may strike from these storms. Local fire departments are asking for citizen help in spoting any possible fires due to lightning strikes.
