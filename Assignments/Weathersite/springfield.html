Springfield braces for possible flooding situations

What to do in a flooding emergancy

As our weather emergancy week here in Springfield draws to a close, today we are going to talk about the dangers of flooding. With the high snowfall that we had this year, our risk of floods happening during the spring thaw are highly elevated.

We have never been a stranger to flooding in our area and this year will prove no different. Already city officials are working to shore up the banks of our local rivers. Our local emergancy responders are running drills in river rescues. But what should you do if you find yourself in a flooding situation?

First do not panic. If at all possible find higher ground, if you are caught in the water try to grasp onto anything that will float and kick your way to the shore. Do not try to fight against the current but move with it.
