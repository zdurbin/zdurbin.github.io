  • April 13-15 - Beginners Tour

    • This ride is designed for those who have little to no mountain biking experience. This ride is approximately 2 miles long and can be completed with very little difficulty. On average this ride lasts for 3 hours on well worn paths. Multiple time slots available. Bicyles are provided. The cost of this trip is $50

  • April 20-22 - Mountain Bike Trails Tour

    • This ride is designed for those who have some mountain biking experience. This ride is approximately 10 miles long and can be completed with little difficulty. On average this ride lasts for 5 hours on both rugged and maintained paths. Multiple time slots available. Bicyles are provided. The cost of this trip is $100

  • May 5 and 6 - Advanced Mountain Trails

    • This ride is designed for those who have advanced mountain biking experience. This ride is approximately 20 miles long and can be completed with little difficulty. On average this ride lasts for 8 hours through rough terrain. Bicycles are NOT provided for this trip. The cost of this trip is $150

  • June 1-10 - Bike Across Montana Tour

    • This tour is our longest tour. The trip will start at 8 am on the first and conclude aproximently at 6pm on the tenth with about 10 hours of biking time a day. The trip starts and ends in Bozeman MT and makes a scenic loop through the state. Accomidations will be provided each night. Breakfast and Dinner is provided as well. The route takes the rider through paved, unpaved, and rugged paths. Limited spaces are available for this trip. The cost of this trip is $1000
