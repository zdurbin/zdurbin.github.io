Overview

Site Purpose

To bring local weather information and news to the users of Franklin, Greenville, and Springfield Counties..

Target Audience

Target age range: 15-100. Target gender: male-female. Target demographic: The local residents of the above-named areas as well as any persons traveling to these areas.
